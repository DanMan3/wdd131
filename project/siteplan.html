Charlie Kirk — A Life Remembered

A memorial site for the community to learn about Charlie Kirk's life and share memories.

Site Name

Charlie Kirk — A Life Remembered — A site focused on remembering the life of Charlie Kirk. I chose it because I was at the event where he was shot and killed, and I wanted to make something to honor him.

Site Purpose

The site provides a respectful, centralized place to share Charlie's biography, memorial service details and leave tributes or condolences.

Scenarios

  • “Who was Charlie Kirk?”
  • “How can I sign the guest book?”
  • “What memorial events are coming up?”
